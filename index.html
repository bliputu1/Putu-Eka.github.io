Gallery

Mount Catur

Mount Catur is a mountain located in Bali, Indonesia. With a height of around 2,096 meters above sea level, this mountain is part of a mountain range in Bali which offers stunning natural views. Trekking to the peak usually takes around 2 to 3 hours.

Mount Batur

Mount Batur is one of the most famous volcanoes in Bali, Indonesia, with a height of approximately 1,717 meters. The climb is done in the early morning to witness the spectacular view of the sunrise.

Trunyan Hill

Trunyan Hill is located on the shores of Lake Batur, famous for its natural beauty and unique culture of the Trunyan people, including the tradition of burial under the Taru Menyan tree.
